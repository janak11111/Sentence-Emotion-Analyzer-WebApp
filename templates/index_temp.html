Introduction

With Tweet Analyzer, you can analyze your tweets and find out if they are happy, sad, or depressing. Simply enter your tweet in the input box on the "Try" page and press the "Analyze" button. Tweet Analyzer uses a trained machine learning model to analyze your tweet and provide you with a sentiment score.
